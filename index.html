About Us

“K U & Company” is a Practicing Company Secretary firm established by Ms. Krati Upadhyay as a sole Proprietorship Firm registered with the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI).

The Firm renders a wide variety of corporate services under Company Law, Limited Liability Partnership, Securities Law and other corporate laws. The keen focus of the firm is to provide the best quality and prompt services to its client with utmost sincerity and passion.

Krati Upadhyay is an Associate member of Institute of Company Secretaries of India and a law graduate from University of Rajasthan, Jaipur with strong academic profile. She has completed her training from a reputed Practicing company Secretary Firm based at Jaipur and was associated with the same firm before establishing K U & Company. She has gained exposure in various segments of company law and other secretarial laws and having good knowledge over Company Secretarial and compliance related matters. She has assisted in the secretarial audits of various Listed and unlisted Companies, including Government and other Companies registered under Special Acts. Owing to firm’s values, she has very transparent business dealings and make comfortable and secured environment for her clients for their confidential data and information.
